Refund & Cancellation Policy

Policy Effective: January 2, 2026

Services Provided by Upside Networks LLC

Upside Networks LLC primarily operates as a technology sourcing advisor and consultant. Our standard service model does not involve direct billing for underlying carrier services or physical products; those agreements are held directly between the client and the chosen provider.

Consulting Fees & Refunds

For most clients, our service is compensated via commission from the chosen provider, meaning there are no direct advisory fees paid to Upside Networks LLC by the client.

In cases where a specific consulting project or audit fee is charged directly to the client:

  • Refunds: Fees are non-refundable once the contracted work has commenced, as our value is delivered through time, analysis, and strategic sourcing efforts.
  • Cancellation: Clients may cancel a defined consulting engagement before the statement of work (SOW) has begun for a full refund of any retainer paid.

Cancellation of Carrier Services

The ability to cancel, terminate, or receive a refund for any service (Internet, VoIP, Cloud, etc.) is governed entirely by the contract and policies of the underlying carrier or provider you selected. Upside Networks LLC is not party to these contracts and cannot authorize refunds or cancellations on their behalf.

We are happy to assist clients in navigating the carrier's cancellation process, but the outcome is solely determined by the provider's contractual terms.

Fee-Based Products

If Upside Networks LLC directly sells a managed service or hardware:

  • Hardware: Refunds for hardware (e.g., routers, VoIP phones) are only permitted if the hardware is returned unused and in original packaging within 30 days of receipt, subject to a 15% restocking fee.
  • Managed Services: Cancellation of monthly managed services requires a minimum of 30 days written notice.
