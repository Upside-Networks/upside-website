Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

Upside Networks LLC, LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and clients who use our advisory and sourcing services. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our services, such as your name, contact details, company name, and email address. This typically occurs via our Contact Us forms.

Usage Data

When you visit our website, our servers automatically record information, including your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and the pages you visited. This data is used for analytics and to improve the user experience.

Payment Data Processing

Upside Networks LLC may require payment for specific consulting services or managed service fees. We take the security of your payment information seriously:

  • No Storage of Credit Card Data: We do not directly collect or store full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or card expiration dates on our servers or in our databases.
  • Third-Party Processor: All payment transactions are processed through a secure, PCI-compliant third-party payment gateway (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, or similar). This processor encrypts and secures the payment data.
  • Tokenization: For recurring billing (if applicable), we use payment tokenization, where a non-sensitive token is stored on our system, which is useless outside of the designated payment processor.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with the requested services and solutions.
  • To respond to your inquiries and offer customer support.
  • To process payments and manage billing for our fee-based services.
  • For business analytics, such as identifying popular solutions.
  • To send marketing and promotional communications, in line with your preferences.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to any third parties.

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as the aforementioned payment processors and email service providers.
  • Technology Partners: Technology vendors and carriers only with your explicit consent for the purpose of obtaining quotes and provisioning services on your behalf.
