The UPSIDE 4-Step Process

Simplifying Complex Telecom Procurement and Management

Our methodology is designed to reduce the time, cost, and risk associated with procuring and managing critical IT infrastructure. We handle the complexity so you can focus on your core business.

1

Discovery & Needs Assessment

We begin with a deep dive into your current technology stack, business objectives, and pain points. We analyze bandwidth needs, geographic requirements, existing contract obligations, and future scalability goals to form a clear strategy.

2

Sourcing & Vetting

Leveraging our network of over 200 carriers and providers, we generate a short-list of the best-fit solutions. We compare proposals based on service level agreements (SLAs), pricing, implementation timelines, and technical compatibility—presenting you with an unbiased, consolidated report.

3

Implementation Management

Once a provider is selected, UPSIDE manages the entire deployment process. We handle order submission, project management, installation scheduling, and coordination between your team and the carrier's technicians, ensuring a smooth, timely cutover.

4

Ongoing Optimization & Support

Our partnership continues long after installation. We provide proactive contract management, rate optimization, service escalations, and regular reviews to ensure your technology stack evolves with your business needs and stays cost-effective.
